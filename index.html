About

Prof. Saed Khayat is a distinguished scholar specializing in water resources management, hydro-geochemistry, and isotope hydrology. He holds a Ph.D. in Hydrochemistry and Isotope Hydrology from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) in Germany, obtained in 2005.

Research Interests

  • Climate change and water resources
  • Hydro-geochemistry
  • Isotopes in the water cycle
  • Wastewater treatment and reuse
  • Saline and brackish water treatment
  • Groundwater pollution monitoring
  • Surface water modeling
  • Irrigation water management

Publications

Prof. Khayat has an extensive publication record, including multiple book chapters and journal articles addressing key environmental challenges.
